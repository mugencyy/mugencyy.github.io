About me
I am from Hong Kong and studied in the United Kingdom for over 8 years and recently studied in UX Design in the Netherlands.

Studing UX gave me a lot of experience in creating real products, understanding the psychology behind UX Patterns. I learnt and understood the research process, creative process, business aspects, wireframing and also prototyping.

I am willing to learn, enthusiastic in design, and striving to be even better than ever and it would be great to meet you, work with you, or just have a chat!
